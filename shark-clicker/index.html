FAQ

Is my progress saved?
Yes. Your progress is saved automatically on your device.
進行状況は端末ごとに自動保存されます。

How do I remove ads?
Buy "Remove Ads" (one-time purchase) on the Extra (⭐) tab. Use "Restore" after changing devices. Optional reward videos stay available after purchase.
「⭐Extra」タブの「Remove Ads（広告を消す・買い切り）」で非表示にできます。機種変更時は「Restore」を。

What is the ×2 boost?
Watching an optional reward video doubles your income for 30 minutes. It is entirely optional.
任意のリワード動画を見ると30分間だけ収入が2倍になります（任意）。
