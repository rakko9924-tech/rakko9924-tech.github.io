Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 18, 2026

This policy explains how "Shark Clicker" (the "App") handles your information.

Information we collect

The App itself does not collect personal information such as your name or email address. Your game progress is stored only on your device.

本アプリ自体は氏名・メールアドレス等の個人情報を収集しません。ゲームの進行状況は端末内にのみ保存されます。

Advertising

The App shows ads via Google AdMob. We do not use App Tracking Transparency and do not request the advertising identifier (IDFA) for tracking, so AdMob serves non-personalized ads. AdMob may still process limited data (such as coarse location and device information) to deliver and measure ads. See the Google Privacy Policy. If you purchase "Remove Ads", banner ads are no longer shown.

本アプリは Google AdMob の広告を表示します。トラッキング（ATT/IDFA）は行わず、非パーソナライズ広告のみ配信します。「Remove Ads」購入後はバナー広告を表示しません。

Purchases

"Remove Ads" is a one-time non-consumable purchase processed by the App Store (StoreKit). Payment is handled by Apple; the App does not store any payment information.

「Remove Ads」はApp Store（StoreKit）経由の買い切り課金です。決済はAppleが処理し、本アプリは決済情報を保持しません。

Children

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children.
